Semantic and Non-Semantic Elements

HTML elements can be categorized into semantic and non-semantic elements:

  • Semantic Elements: Clearly describe their meaning in a human- and machine-readable way (e.g., <header> , <footer> , <article>, <nav>, <aside>, <details>, <figcaption>, <figure>, <main>, <section>, <summary>, <time>, ).
  • Non-Semantic Elements: Do not provide any information about the content they contain (e.g., <div>, <span>).
